Naruto

In a nutshell, Naruto is about the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja, who strives to become the Hokage (leader) of his village and gain the respect and recognition he's always yearned for.

One Piece

One Piece tells the story of Monkey D. Luffy's journey to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure, One Piece. He assembles a crew and sails the Grand Line, facing pirates and the World Government.

Demon Slayer

In Taisho-era Japan, Tanjiro fights demons after his family is slaughtered. His sister, Nezuko, becomes a demon, and Tanjiro seeks a way to cure her and avenge his family.

Death Note

In the world of Death Note, a Shinigami drops a notebook with the power to kill anyone whose name is written in it. Light Yagami, a genius student, discovers the notebook and uses it to cleanse the world of criminals, believing himself to be a savior.
