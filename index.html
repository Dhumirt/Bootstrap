Animal

The movie "Animal" is a Hindi-language action thriller about a son's destructive path after his father's assassination attempt, exploring their strained relationship. The film is directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga and stars Ranbir Kapoor. .

Pushpa 2: The Rule

Pushpa 2: The Rule is an Indian Telugu action drama sequel following Pushpa Raj's rise. It stars Allu Arjun, Rashmika Mandanna, and Fahadh Faasil. The movie centers on Pushpa's fight against Inspector Shekhawat and other rivals. .

Money Heist

In the heist drama Money Heist, a mastermind, the Professor, leads a team to rob the Royal Mint of Spain, holding hostages and manipulating the police. They plan and execute a complex operation, aiming for a monumental haul.

Stanger Things

In the 1980s, a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana, face supernatural threats when a boy vanishes and a mysterious girl with powers appears. They uncover a government lab and fight creatures from the Upside Down.
